# Customer Behavior Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes retail customer shopping behavior using Python (EDA), SQL, and Power BI. The goal is to identify patterns in customer demographics, product categories, discounts, and purchasing behavior to support better business decisions.

---

## Problem Statement
A retail company wants to understand customer behavior across demographics and product categories to improve sales, customer engagement, and retention strategies.

---

## Dataset
- Source: customer_shopping_behavior.csv
- Records: 3,900 transactions
- Features: 18 attributes
- Total Revenue: $233,081
- Average Purchase Value: $59.76
- Average Review Rating: 3.75

Key columns:
customer_id, age, gender, category, purchase_amount, review_rating, subscription_status, shipping_type, discount_applied, previous_purchases

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) – Data cleaning & EDA
- SQL (MySQL) – Business analysis
- Power BI – Dashboard & visualization

---

## Project Workflow

### 1. Exploratory Data Analysis (Python)
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Feature engineering (age groups)
- Trend analysis and visualization
- Customer segmentation

### 2. SQL Analysis
Key business questions:
- Revenue by gender
- Top-performing products
- Discount impact on spending
- Customer segmentation (new, returning, loyal)
- Subscriber vs non-subscriber behavior

### 3. Power BI Dashboard
- KPI cards (Revenue, Customers, Avg Purchase, Ratings)
- Category-wise performance
- Age-group analysis
- Subscription distribution
- Interactive filters

---

## Key Insights
- Clothing is the top revenue-generating category
- Repeat buyers contribute the highest revenue
- Discounts influence high-spending customers
- Young adults generate the most revenue
- 73% of customers are non-subscribers

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing on high-performing categories
- Convert repeat buyers into subscribers
- Use targeted discounts instead of blanket offers
- Personalize campaigns using demographic insights
- Promote high-rated products
